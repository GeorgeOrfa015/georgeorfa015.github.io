Latest news
Friday, November 15, 2024 - Added a new project: Cable Voltage Drop! It's a calculator designed for finding out what is the voltage drop of a cable of set length and cross-sectional area when Current is flowing through. It also calculates other useful metrics for that cable, like the efficiency and resistance of the wire.
